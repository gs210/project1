About The Band

Founded in Medford, Massachusetts, Medford Midlines is a four-piece indie rock cover band known for dynamic performances and crowd-driven energy. Each member brings a distinct musical background, creating a sound that is both tight and expressive.

Specializing in modern alternative favorites and timeless indie anthems, the band performs throughout New England at weddings, corporate events, and live music venues. Their mission is simple: deliver high-quality musicianship with the kind of energy that keeps audiences engaged from the first chord to the final encore.

Alex Jefferson

Lead Vocals & Rhythm Guitar

With a voice built for indie anthems and packed dance floors, Alex anchors the Midlines' sound. Drawing inspiration from The Killers and Arctic Monkeys, he delivers every performance with intensity and connection.

Joe Washington

Lead Guitar

Joe's brings layered textures and soaring riffs that define the band's dynamic live sound. His background in alternative and festival circuits gives the Midlines their signature edge.

Harry Hamilton

Bass

Driving grooves and tight rhythms keep every set moving. Harry's steady presence locks in the foundation that powers both high-energy covers and melodic slow builds.

Louis Lincoln

Drums

Louis delivers precision and punch behind the kit, bringing festival-level energy to every venue. His dynamic style gives the band its unmistakable live intensity.
